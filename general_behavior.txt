Team layout:

	- 1 Goalie
		- blow on sight of blue team or ball
		- play music

	- 2 attackers:
		- Find ball
		- Find goal

	- 2 defenders:
		- Find ball
		- Push/blow ball towards other goal
		- Limit movement
